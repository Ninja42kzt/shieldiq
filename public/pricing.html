Why Pro gates the AI?

Our AI engine analyses individual quiz answers, identifies patterns in mistakes, and generates personalised training paths. This requires significant compute — Pro keeps it sustainable while keeping Free genuinely useful.

Common Questions

Can I stay on Free forever?

Yes. Free includes all training modules with no time limit. You only need Pro for AI features and admin tools.

How does per-user pricing work?

You pay $12/month for each team member on Pro. One admin can manage billing for the whole company.

Can I cancel anytime?

Yes. Cancel at any time — your team drops to Free at the end of the billing period with no penalties.

Is there a trial?

The Free plan is essentially an unlimited trial. Upgrade when your team needs the AI and admin features.
