Why does AI require Business or Enterprise?

Our neural network analyses every quiz answer, identifies patterns in mistakes across your team, and generates personalised training paths per employee. This requires significant compute resources — Business and Enterprise plans keep it sustainable while ensuring Free is genuinely useful for individuals.

Common Questions

Can I stay on Free forever?

Yes. Free includes 3 modules with no time limit. Upgrade only when your team needs AI features, admin tools or more modules.

How does per-user pricing work?

You pay per active user per month. Business caps at 10 users ($80 max/mo). Enterprise caps at 30 users ($450 max/mo). Add or remove users anytime.

What's the yearly discount?

Pay annually and save 20% — Business drops to $6.40/user/mo and Enterprise to $12/user/mo. Billed as one annual payment.

Can I switch plans anytime?

Yes. Upgrade instantly, downgrade at the end of your billing period. No penalties or hidden fees.

Do you offer discounts for NGOs or schools?

Yes — contact us at hello@shieldiq.com for education and nonprofit pricing. We believe everyone deserves cybersecurity awareness.

What payment methods do you accept?

M-PESA, Visa, Mastercard, and bank transfer for Enterprise. More East African payment options coming soon.
